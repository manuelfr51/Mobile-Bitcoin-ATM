If you already have a Blockchain, Coinbase, or Bitstamp wallet, you can link it to your account by adding your API credentials below. That being said, we recommend that all of our users create a separate wallet to use with CoinSafe that holds a smaller amount of funds that they can top off whenever the balance gets low.
